# SMAP: Semantic Route Planning with Map-Grounded Multimodal Alignment

**IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2026**

---

## Authors

**Wenjie Zhang\***, Yang Chen\*, Lu Xin, Wang Zhen, Liu Yue, Xi Bobo, Zhang Pengbo

<small>\* Equal contribution</small>

---

## Abstract

SMAP is the first multimodal framework for semantic route planning that integrates user queries, POI metadata, and map tiles to generate spatially coherent itineraries while significantly reducing geographical hallucinations through a novel self-editing and preference optimization pipeline.
